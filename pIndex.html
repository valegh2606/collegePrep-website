Pre-College Programs

Pre-college summer programs are a great opportunity to experience college life.
The following universities offer fully funded pre-college programs, which means they are completely free, and merit based.
Make sure you submit your application before the deadline. Some universisties ask you to select the scholarship option once you complete your application.

AI Scholars, Computer Science Scholars, and the
Summer Academy for Math and Science are fully
funded. The university also offers scholarships for the
other pre-college programs.

Many universities also offer scholarships to pay for the total cost of pre-college programs.
